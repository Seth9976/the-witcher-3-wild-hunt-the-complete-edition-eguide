The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Official Digital Strategy Guide

Main Quest: The Play's The Thing To Do a Great Right Do a Little Wrong

Objectives:

  • Head to the docks and hire ushers for the play.
  • (Optional) Defeat the strongmen form Metinna.
  • Nearest Signpost: Novigrad Docks

Travel west toward the southern part of Novigrad docks, where the rowdy townsfolk and dockhands live. Follow the curve of the dockside until you reach the commotion of a street fight. Strike up a conversation with a sizable gentleman called Sofus the Bull. Tell him you require his services to provide protection for the play. But Sofus and his brother Hal are only interested in arse-kicking. You can:

  • (70 Crowns) Offer to pay him for his services. He agrees.
  • (Fistfight) Or agree to a bout with Sofus and Hal by the docks. Utilize fists only, and prepare for fisticuffs. Winning or losing the bout still results in Sofus, Hal, and his toughs agreeing to help you.
