La Cage Au Fou Could’ve Left The Door Unlatched…

Related Quests: Main Quest: Blood Run, Main Quest: Where Children Toil, Toys Waste Away; Secondary Quest: Spoontaneous Profits!, Secondary Quest: No Place Like Home, Secondary Quest: The Hunger Game

Locations: Dockside Warehouse (Unmarked), Mere-Lachaiselongue Cemetery, Trastamara Hunting Cottage, Trastamara Manor (Unmarked), Tesham Mutna

Characters: Emiel Regis Rohellec Terzieff-Godefroy, Wight: Spoon Collector, Marlene Trastamara, Barnabas-Basil Foulty, Count de la Croix

Enemies: Ghouls, Alghouls, Panthers, Archespores, Kikimore Workers, Kikimore Warrior, Barghests, Wight: Spoon Collector, Rotfiends, Scurvers, Fleders, Katakans

Objectives

  • Meet Regis at Mere-Lachaiselongue Cemetery.
  • Find the entrance to the crypt.

QUESTING DECISIONS

This quest is accessible immediately once you have completed Blood and Wine: Main Quest: Blood Run. Feel free to inspect the Corvo Bianco winery that you now own (Secondary Quest: No Place Like Home) if you wish, and begin any optional explorations of Toussaint.

Journey to the Mere-Lachaiselongue Cemetery. Regis isn’t here to welcome you. In fact, he’s nowhere to be seen. Instead, contend with some roaming Ghouls, a possible Panther attack, and a few disgusting, snake and plant-like foes known as Archespores. The scavengers around the small chapel leading to the crypt aren’t your main focus; getting into the crypt is. Which is tricky as Regis isn’t answering the chapel door. It seems you need to find another way into the catacombs under the graveyard.

CUTTING OFF THE HEAD OF THE SNAKE

These poisonous Archespore pods and their snake-like heads like to burrow, spit, and thrash with violence and cunning. Consult the Bestiary for the optimal tactics when attempting to weed out this carnivorous plant life, along with any prowling Panthers if this is your first encounter with big cats.

Once you’re into the crypt, prepare to face a group of scuttling Kikimores. They have some tactics to try too. See the Bestiary for further details.

Move into the investigation area on your map, and begin a thorough inspection of the above-ground tombs until you stumble upon a large carved crypt opening (as shown). The tomb is open, allowing you to vault over, and drop into a small rocky tunnel. Draw your sword and engage a cluster of Kikimores Workers, revolting scuttling foes that inhabit this dark, dank place. Feel free to burn the clusters of large egg sacs to earn additional XP before facing a more powerful Kikimore Warrior. Then continue roughly south until you reach an opening into the main crypt itself. From here, it is a short stroll through the catacombs up and into the lair of Regis.

CUTTING OFF THE HEAD OF THE SNAKE

Your Eye of Nehaleni may be vibrating, once you’re through the Kikimore nest, but before meeting Regis. Use it on a wall-like illusion, leading to a hidden crypt with a choice Relic sword and documentation about who is buried here.
