The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Official Digital Strategy Guide

Training Controls

Geralt’s search for Ciri and the Wild Hunt takes him across plains, and through forests, castles, and mountains. He travels by foot, on horseback, and even by boat. He also needs to fight monsters, men, and men who would be monsters if the choice was theirs. Learn how to control Geralt to the fullest, so you’re not fumbling with the controls when a pack of Drowners swarms you.
