Iron Maiden Run To The Hills

Location: Ringvold

Characters: Jutta an Dimun, Gundar

Enemies: None

Objectives

  • Nearest Signpost: Hills east of Ringvold.

While in the Faroe village of Ringvold, find the path heading east out of the settlement, and follow it up the nearby hills to the top of the peak, where the Skellige warriors spar. Step into the ring, and speak with Jutta an Dimun, a formidable swordswoman. No man has bested her in combat. Ask to fight, and she ponders this:

  • She challenges you straight away if you slew the leader of the slavers, after completing Secondary Quest: Flesh for Sale.
  • She challenges you immediately if you bettered Einar of Faroe during Secondary Quest: Fists of Fury: Skellige.

Otherwise, your deeds aren’t known to her, and you’ll need to prove your mettle first. She tells you one of two tasks you must complete:

  • She’ll face you if you defeat Gundar, back in town.
  • Or, she’ll face you if you dive into the ocean, and bring back an old sword from a sunken ship.
