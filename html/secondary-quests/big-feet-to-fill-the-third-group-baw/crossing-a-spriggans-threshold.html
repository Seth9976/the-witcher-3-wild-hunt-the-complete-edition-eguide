Big Feet To Fill: The Third Group (Blood and Wine) Crossing A Spriggan’s Threshold

Related Quests: Secondary Quest: Big Feet to Fill

Locations: Lebioda’s Footsteps (Location #3)

Characters: Worker

Enemies: Wolves, Spriggan

Objectives

  • Examine the marks around the abandoned wagons.
  • Follow the worker’s footprints.

At a crossroads close to an inlet shore of the river, almost due north of Castel Ravello Vineyard, is a right mess, signs of carnage and three prowling wolves to slay. Afterward, an examination of the area is in order, using your Witcher Senses. Something strong smashed the horse’s bones. A second horse is ripped to shreds. And there are tracks in the long grass; deep prints and blood; someone was trying to get away. Follow the prints heading roughly southeast, until you stumble into a Spriggan attempting to burrow into a dirt mound.

Objectives

  • Kill the Spriggan.
  • Explore the entrance to the cavity.

This fearsome monster of the woods turns its attention on you! Face this frightening foe in combat, learning the techniques detailed of the Bestiary if you need help defeating it. With the Spriggan cut down, use your Witcher Senses to inspect the burrow in the ground the beast was interested in. You coax a worker out, and complete this task.
