Wine Wars: The Deus In The Machina (Baw) Taking Stock Of The Archespore Saplings

Locations: Cooper’s Cottage (Unmarked), Vermentino Vineyard (Unmarked)

Characters: Augustine Tonneliay

Enemies: Rotfiends

QUESTING DECISIONS: WHAT THE DEUS?!

This optional quest begins when you’ve made a thorough sweep of both the Guarded Treasure locations during Wine Wars: Coronata and Wine Wars: Vermentino. You need to have acquired and read all the letters (the Orders on elegant stationery, and the Letter on elegant stationery). When the information is correlated, and the brass key from the corpse you found the elegant letter is obtained, this quest commences.

Objectives

  • Search the warehouse using your Witcher Senses.
  • Defeat the archespore.
  • Talk to Liam and Matilda.

QUESTING DECISIONS

This Treasure Hunt is only available during Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Vermentino.
