Secondary Quests: Take What You Want Teigr, Teigr, Burning Bright

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Official Digital Strategy Guide

Secondary Quests: Take What You Want Teigr, Teigr, Burning Bright

Related Quests: Contract: The Beast of Honorton, Secondary Quest: Where the Cat and Wolf Play…

Locations: Roadside Shrine (Unmarked), Oreton

Characters: Witcher Gaetan

Enemies: Bandits

Objectives

  • Go to the School of the Cat Witcher’s stash to collect your reward.

QUESTING DECISIONS

This Quest starts automatically if you spare the life of Witcher Gaetan towards the end of Secondary Quest: Where the Cat and Wolf Play…

North of Midcopse, and in the woods due north of Keira Metz’s hut, is a forested glade with an old oak tree. Approach the cluster of rocks close by, where you may encounter a group of three bandits inspecting a hole in the ground. After Bandit slaughter, check that area too; it’s a tunnel down to the stash itself. Inside is a cot, a group of three wall trophies (of a foglet, ghoul, and grave hag) to inspect, and a chest containing the following rewards. As the quest title suggests, take what you want. The quest concludes when you open Gaetan’s chest.

Items: Crowns (250), Letter to Gaetan, Teigr (Steel Sword)
