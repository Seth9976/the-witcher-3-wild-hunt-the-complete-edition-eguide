Don’t Take Candy From A Stranger (Baw) Skullduggery At The Winery

As part of Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Vermentino, you must dispatch a trio of Rotfiends from the Cooper’s Cottage, north of Castel Ravello Vineyard. Once the area is secured, inspect the corpse between the cottage and the old oak tree. Search the body for a journal and a key; read the missive to discover there’s a stash of coin hidden somewhere in a sunflower field.

Related Quests: Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Belgaard (Part 2), Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Coronata, Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Vermentino

Locations: Tesham Mutna, Abandoned Storehouse (Unmarked)

Characters: Ducal Clerk, Liam de Coronata, Matilda of Vermentino

Enemies: Archespore

Objectives

  • Find the place mentioned in the letter.

Due east of the ruins of Tesham Mutna, in the far southeast of Toussaint, is an Abandoned Storehouse in Caroberta Woods. The Archespore saplings that were planted on both Liam and Matilda’s lands should be somewhere around here. Begin your search by looking for the doorway under the wall, leading to the warehouse cellar.

Objectives

  • Read the Augustine Tonnelay’s journal.

Items: Augustine the Cooper’s Journal, Key to chest in sunflower fields
