The Price Of Honor Blue And Bloated

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Official Digital Strategy Guide

The Price Of Honor Blue And Bloated

Location: Ringvold, northeast coast of Faroe

Characters: Timmon, Adga, Mikkel, Ulf

Enemies: Drowned Dead, Sirens

Recommended Items: Keira’s Magic Lamp

Objectives

  • Examine the coasts of Faroe on the side facing Hindarsfjall.
  • Nearest Signpost: East of Trottheim.

You are beckoned by a young burly man named Timmon, who is worried about his betrothed and brothers. Mikkel and Ulf sailed to Hindarsfjall a few days ago to fetch Agda for a wedding, but they haven’t returned, and he fares they may have run aground along the coast of Faroe. Agree to search for the missing wedding party.

Questing Decisions

This quest is accessible at any time, but can only be completed after you obtain the Magic Lamp from Keira, during Secondary Quest: Magic Lamp.

Objectives

  • Use your Witcher Senses to search the shore.
  • Examine the beach near the dead girl using the magic lamp.

Venture by foot or by boat to the northeastern shores of Faroe, east of Trottheim to the rocky and wooded shoreline. Expect drowned dead and sirens to be found in this area.

Slay at least four of each. After clearing the objective area of foes, use your Witcher Senses to more easily discover the bloated remains of a dead girl. Alas, this must be Adga. But there are others here—spirits that you must listen to.

Produce the magic lamp, and two ghosts appear, forlorn and hovering above the body. These are Mikkel and Ulf, who despite their best efforts, failed to save Adga from the storm. They cannot face returning to their brethren, so they decide to end it all.

Objectives

  • Follow the footsteps.
  • Investigate the crime scene.
  • Tell the young man what happened to his betrothed and his brothers.

Use your Witcher Senses to uncover two sets of footprints, and follow them southeast, across the beach and up the hill to a beautiful clifftop promontory where two bodies lie slumped against a tree. One carries the following items, and both seem to have taken their own lives.

Items: Quest Items: Chest Key, Letter from Agda’s father to Timmon

The chest key opens a chest inside the bride’s sunken vessel, close to where you found Agda’s body. Now return to Timmon, and inform him of the sad news (keeping or giving him any crowns you found in the chest, if you opened it). His grief is immense. The quest then concludes. Open the chest afterwards too, if you haven’t already.

Items: Crowns
