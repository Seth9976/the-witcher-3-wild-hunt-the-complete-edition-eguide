The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Official Digital Strategy Guide

Wine Wars: Consorting (Baw) Strange Bedfellows

Related Quests: Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Coronata, Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Vermentino, Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: The Deus in the Machina, Secondary Quest: Wine Wars: Belgaard (Part 2)

Locations: Coronata Vineyard (and surroundings), Vermentino Vineyard (and surroundings), Belgaard Vineyard

Characters: Ducal Clerk, Liam de Coronata, Matilda of Vermentino

Objectives

  • Help Liam and Matilda’s consortium (10).
  • Return to Liam and Matilda.

QUESTING DECISIONS

This Quest begins once one of the following has occurred:

You have completed Wine Wars: The Deus in the Machina and spoken to the Ducal Clerk, who awards the Belgaard winery to both Liam and Matilda.

This quest automatically replaces Coronata and Vermentino quests if they have not been finished yet before Wine Wars: The Deus in the Machina is concluded.

Over at the Belgaard Vineyard it seems both Liam and Matilda understand that working together, especially with all the additional troubles the Belgaard winery is experiencing, is the only course of action. However, they still require all of the problems in their respective vineyards to be solved. If you haven’t solved the five problems in Coronata, and the five problems in Vermentino, now is the time to attempt them. When both their territories are restored to working order, you are paid your agreed sum, and this quest concludes.
