The Four Faces Of Hemdall* A Wanderer With A Purpose

Locations: Shrine to Hemdall (Faroe)

Characters: Pilgrim

Enemies: Pirates

Objectives

  • Nearest Signpost: Northwest of Trottheim.

Although you may have more to worry about that a motley band of thugs stopping the pilgrimage of a druid, you should still assist the wise man, in the hilly region between the two villages. Beware of endriaga in the immediate area, before closing in on the discussion between three pirates and the pilgrim. You can ignore the pilgrim’s plight, or force the pirates to let him through to the shrine they are guarding. They aren’t about to, forcing you to slay all three of them.

Afterward, you can speak to the pilgrim about his journey to the Four Faces of Hemdall, and the forthcoming prophecies. Then he bids you farewell and sits at the shrine. Afterwards, you may run into him again, at one of the other three shrines on Faroe. Can you find them all? One of them can’t be accessed by the druid until you’ve removed Hammond from it, during Secondary Quest: Following the Thread.
