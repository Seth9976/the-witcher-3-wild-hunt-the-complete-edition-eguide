Of Sheers And A Witcher I Sing (Blood and Wine) Whose Singing It Droned On And On…

Objectives

  • Use your Witcher Senses to look for clues about what happened to Jean-Louis.

Conduct a thorough search of the interior of “A Cut Above” Barber’s shop, beginning with the customer chairsand mirrors:

  • There are empty pomade jars; they stink more than La Papillon’s poetry
  • The scissors are dull; they haven’t cut hair for a while.

Head upstairs to continue your search:

  • The chest at the foot of the bed hasn’t been touched, so there’s no forced entry here. There’s another empty jar of pomade, too.
  • Over by the window is a coiffer’s log listing hair styles long out of favor in Novigrad. His last client was a Lady le Pompidou, but there are no leads there. Of greater interest is a map sketch; it seems to indicate (after you request Le Papillon shut up) the barber went out to find ingredients for his pomade. Gather the map.

Items: Jean-Louis Ludovic’s Map
