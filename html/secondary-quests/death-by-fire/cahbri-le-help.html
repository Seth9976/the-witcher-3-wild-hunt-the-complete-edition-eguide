Death By Fire Ca’hbri Le! Help!

Location: Codgers’ Quarry

Characters: Elven Victim

Enemies: Bandits

Objectives

  • Defeat the bandits.
  • Clear the blockage.
  • Talk to the survivor.
  • Nearest Signpost: Codger’s Quarry.

On the pathway around the rim of Codger’s Quarry (to the west) you should see smoke, and a burning cottage with a group of bandits standing outside it. They aren’t receptive to reason, so you’re left with the following choices:

  • Ignoring the situation.
  • Slaughtering four foes before the elf they’ve trapped inside the structure is burned alive. Free the elf, and she assists with your combat against the bandits, using a bow.
  • Speaking to the bandits and choosing not to get involved before using Aard to remove the blockade, releasing the elf who assists you with a bow.

Break into the structure with Aard, and the elf will pick up a bow and help you. Speak to the elf who was about to be burned alive by these thugs. But don’t half-listen to her thanks because she mentions a hollowed-out stump behind the house where her valuables are hidden. You may take whatever you wish from it as the quest concludes.

Items: 200 Orens
