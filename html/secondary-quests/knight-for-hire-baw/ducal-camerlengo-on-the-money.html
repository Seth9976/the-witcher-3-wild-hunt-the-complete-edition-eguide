STARTING THIS SECONDARY QUEST

Occasionally, when you’ve adventured through Toussaint and completed a particularly heroic deed, you receive a message entitled either “Reward from the Ducal Camerlengo” or “Knight for Hire”. The following information details exactly what deeds you can undertake, and what rewards to expect. You can also start this quest by visiting the Ducal Camerlengo himself, in the Knights Dormant Square (Beauclair).

Objectives

  • Complete the contracts you took on from the Camerlengo (15).
  • (Optional): Go see the Ducal Camerlengo to collect your pay.

The Ducal Camerlengo, who monitors bandit activity and pays Knights Errant for protecting the Duchy of Toussaint, has his offices on Knights Dormant Square in Beauclair. Look for the building with the sign bearing the same name, in the square close to The Gran’place, across the palace bridge (eastern side). Step into the Ducal’s office and speak with him. The Ducal sells rare books, is proficient at gwent (beat him to claim the King Bram card), and can give you additional information on the coin he pays the knights. Visit him whenever you like. Request coin from him once you’ve performed any of the 15 heroic deeds (which each net you 100 Crowns).
