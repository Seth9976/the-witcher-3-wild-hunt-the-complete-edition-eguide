Inventory Data Decoctions

Decoctions work like Runestones and Glyphs, only they directly enhance Geralt's stats. When boosting your performance, it is important to remember that Geralt can only withstand a limited amount of toxicity. You will not be able to enhance your abilities beyond this threshold.

HOW TO USE

Decoctions are equipped by drinking them, the same as Potions.

HOW TO REMOVE

Once added, Decoctions can be removed by drinking White Honey.

HOW TO REFILL

Decoctions can be replenished during Meditation by using Strong Alcohols that refill all alchemical items.

WHERE TO FIND

Decoctions cannot be purchased. Instead, a Witcher must craft them using alchemical formulae found in containers or purchased from Alchemist and Herbalist shops throughout the world.

HOW TO CRAFT

Decoctions can be made at any time but may require rare herbs and monster mutagens that may be difficult to acquire during early stages of the game. Creating decoctions can crafted using the alchemy panel to combine components based on the given recipe.
