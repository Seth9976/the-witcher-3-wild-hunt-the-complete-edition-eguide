Inventory Data Foods

DRINKS

Drinks can be consumed to quickly regain stamina. Natural drinks like Juices and Milk will provide the most hydration. The higher the number in the "Effect" column, the more potent the effect (for both Food and Drink).

FOOD

Food can be eaten to produce a small amount of Health regeneration. Wheat and Fruits will provide some amount of healing while Vegatables, Dairy, Meat and Fish produce the greatest amount of vitality.

ALCHEMY

Some edibles can also be used in alchemical formulas and to replenish potions.

COLLECTIBLE

Collectible food items can be sold for an additional price at herbalist and alchemist shops.

TOXICITY

While some Alcohols provide a hydration bonus, they also have a toxic effect on Geralt and may result in blurred vision for a short duration.
