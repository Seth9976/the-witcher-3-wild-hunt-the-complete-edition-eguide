Skellige Clans Deck Skellige Clans Deck: Leader Cards (2)

King Bran

Units only lose half their values in bad weather conditions.

Won at the Toussaint Tournament (The Pheasantry Inn, Beauclair) during Gwent Quest: To Everything – Turn, Turn, Tournament!

OR, Won from the Ducal Camerlengo (near The Gran’place, Beauclair).

Crach an Craite

Shuffles graveyards (discard pile) back into main decks (for both players).

Base Deck (Blood and Wine): You start with this card once you meet Count Monnier at the beginning of Gwent Quest: To Everything – Turn, Turn, Tournament!.
