Scenes From a Marriage A Eulogy, And Into The Painted World

Objectives

  • Find a fitting spot to bury Iris.

You now have the run of the manor. Retrace your steps down the main staircase, and out the main doors (you can now unlock). Into the gloomy garden, you should inspect the following locations as a suitable resting place for Iris. Your previous search of the grounds allows you to easily find the following areas:

  • The bench near the fountain. Iris liked to sit here, but this isn’t a good place for a grave; it is too close to the house.
  • The flower bed. Though Iris adored flowers, she would never want to destroy them. Dig somewhere else.
  • The easel on the raised garden under the large oak tree (roughly northwest of the main manor) seems suitable; you are told Iris loved to paint.

You dig the grave by the easel, and say a few kind words about Iris (choose whatever you wish to say). You can then try to summon her, and place one of the following items you found during your previous searching (it doesn’t matter which, you don’t have to place anything):

  • The sketchpad from the ground floor study and library.
  • The painting of Iris and Olgierd from Iris’ bedchamber.
  • The ornamented hairbrush found in the back garden.

You can also ask the Black Cat and Dog to add their own eulogies. With the remains interred, you attempt to summon her. Her wraith appears, silently walks over to the easel, conjures a painting of the manor, and steps through. The figure of Iris appears in the painting, prompting you to follow her into the Painted World.
